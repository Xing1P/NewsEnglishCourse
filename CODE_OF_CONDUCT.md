# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want News English Course to be a welcoming project for learners, teachers, and developers. We pledge to make participation respectful and constructive, regardless of experience level, nationality, language, background, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, patient, and specific.
- Assume good intent while still asking for clarity when needed.
- Give feedback about ideas and code, not personal traits.
- Respect different levels of English and technical experience.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Public sharing of private contact details, learner data, or private article content without permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption after maintainers ask for redirection.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project community.

To report a concern, email `phanpiseth9@gmail.com`. Reports will be handled with care and as much confidentiality as practical.
